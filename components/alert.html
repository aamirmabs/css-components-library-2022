Alerts

Simple Alerts

Alerts come in multiple colors and can be set by using the appropriate name from primary, secondary, success, danger, warning, info, light and dark. Alerts can also be used without any color and will default to a gray background with black text.

A simple alert!
A primary alert!

A success alert!
A danger alert!
A warning alert!
A info alert!

                <div class="alert">A simple alert!</div>
                <div class="alert alert-primary">A primary alert!</div>
                <div class="alert alert-secondary">A secondary alert!</div>
                <div class="alert alert-success">A success alert!</div>
                <div class="alert alert-danger">A danger alert!</div>
                <div class="alert alert-warning">A warning alert!</div>
                <div class="alert alert-info">A info alert!</div>

Alerts with links

Alerts that contain links in them will have a higher font-weight and be decorated with an underline.
